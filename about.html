About LushCrete.

Our Story

Lush Crete was founded by Alex Powell, a master concreter with over a decade of hands-on experience. Based in Adelaide, Alex started the company with a simple mission: to bring a higher standard of craftsmanship, reliability, and professionalism to every project.

We believe that concrete is more than just a material—it's the foundation of your home and the canvas for your living spaces. It demands respect, precision, and an understanding of its potential. From structurally sound slabs essential for stability, to the beautifully finished driveways that welcome you home, we approach every task with dedication.

Our commitment is unwavering: deliver high-quality work that not only looks exceptional upon completion but also endures the elements and the test of time, adding lasting value to your property.
